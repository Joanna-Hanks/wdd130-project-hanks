German Pancakes

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 6 eggs, lightly beaten
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

Preheat the oven to 425°F. Place the butter in a 9×13-inch baking dish and melt it in the oven. In a medium bowl, mix together the flour, eggs, milk, and salt. Pour the batter into the hot, buttered dish and bake for 20–30 minutes, or until puffed and golden.

Oreos From Heaven

Ingredients

  • 1 package Oreos
  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese
  • (Optional) candy quick or dipping chocolate

Directions

Finely crush the Oreos and mix them thoroughly with the cream cheese until smooth. Roll the mixture into small balls and place them on a cookie sheet, then freeze until firm. For an optional finishing touch, dip the chilled balls in melted chocolate and freeze again until set.
